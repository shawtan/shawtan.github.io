About Me

I am a computer science student currently studying at the University of Waterloo. I grew up in Toronto, where I decided to teach myself how to make a program. Ever since then, the field of computer science has interested me and I always want to learn and improve my technical skills.

Since my first Visual Basic project, I have developed multiple web and standalone applications using Java, HTML5 and Roby on Rails. At hackathons I have worked with Java and Swift to create apps for Android and iOS. In terms of backend, I have worked with PL/SQL, PostgreSQL and Firebase databases.

In my spare time, I like making origami pieces. Lately I have been interested in making geometric solids using modular origami. I also love reading books and watching TV shows. I enjoy sci-fi and fantasy novels and I am currently watching Hannibal, Arrow, Dominion and Suits.
